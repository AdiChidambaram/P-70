News

Iconic Leadership Awards 2020 A proud moment for HFSI The Principal of Hiranandani Foundation School International, Mrs. Kalyani Patnaik has been recognised for her exemplary contribution in the arena of academics by the Hon’ble Governor of Maharashtra, Shri Bhagat Singh Koshyari on 12th September 2020. The programme has been presided over by the President and Chairperson of ISDI/ISMI and former Sheriff of Mumbai, Dr. Indu Shahani. It is a matter of immense prestige and unprecedented honour for the school that the Principal, Mrs. Kalyani Patnaik has been selected to become the proud recipient of the “ICONIC LEADERSHIP AWARD” this year. The award comes as a recognition for the “Exemplary Service and Dedication” in the field of education. The school is striding forward with gigantic steps and the recognition comes at an appropriate moment when the school is overcoming challenges set forth by the global pandemic in the fittest possible way. The leadership of the school is on worthy shoulders and Mrs. Kalyani Patnaik is tirelessly working to enrich and empower the faculty to grapple with online teaching.
